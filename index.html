Welcome to Access Justice, your trusted online resource for understanding
and navigating the laws and rights in the Philippines. Our platform is designed

actions when it comes to their rights and legal matters.

ABOUT

Republic Act No. 10627
Anti-Bullying Act of 2013

Anti-Bullying Act of 2013
Requires schools to create policies against bullying, including procedures for reporting and investigating incidents, both in-person and online.

Learn More
Republic Act No. 10533
Enhanced Basic Education Act of 2013 

Expands the basic education cycle to 12 years, improving the quality and accessibility of education in the Philippines.

Learn More
Republic Act No. 7659
Death Penalty Law (1993)

This law previously provided for capital punishment for heinous crimes, including murder and drug trafficking. It remains relevant in terms of understanding severe penalties for serious criminal offenses.

Learn More
Republic Act No. 9165
Comprehensive Dangerous Drugs Act of 2002

This law governs the possession, manufacture, sale, and trafficking of illegal drugs in the Philippines. It sets harsh penalties for drug-related offenses, including life imprisonment or death (prior to the abolition of the death penalty).

Learn More
Republic Act No. 9003
Ecological Solid Waste Management Act of 2000

This law mandates the proper segregation, collection, and disposal of solid waste. It requires local government units (LGUs) to establish waste management systems and promotes recycling to minimize waste and reduce environmental impact.

Learn More
Republic Act No. 8749
Philippine Clean Air Act of 1999

This law seeks to improve air quality by regulating air pollution, establishing emission standards for vehicles and industries, and promoting cleaner technologies to reduce harmful emissions.

Learn More
Republic Act No. 9372
Human Security Act of 2007

This anti-terrorism law gives the government power to combat terrorism but has been controversial due to concerns that it may infringe on the civil liberties of individuals, such as freedom of expression and assembly.

Learn More
Republic Act No. 11188
Magna Carta of the Poor (2019) 

This law promotes the rights of the poor to access health, education, housing, and other basic services. It aims to address socio-economic inequalities and protect the rights of marginalized sectors from exploitation.

Learn More
Republic Act No. 9745
Anti-Torture Act of 2009

This law prohibits torture and other cruel, inhuman, or degrading treatment or punishment. It provides victims with legal remedies and seeks to prevent human rights abuses, particularly by law enforcement and government officials
